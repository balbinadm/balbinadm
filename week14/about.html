Meet Balbina

Balbina De Silva is a New York based artist and graphic designer currently studying at Parsons the New School for Design. Balbina focuses her artistic practice on stippling techniques and creating hyperrealistic compositions. Aside from fine arts, she is passionate about graphic design -specifically branding, and identity design. Balbina has a bold personality, enjoys a creative challenge, and is always looking to learn, grow, and expand as a person and as a designer.
