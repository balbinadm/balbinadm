What is the purpose of the website?

The purpose of this website will be to showcase my portfolio in in a simple yet striking way. The website will allow me to apply for design internships and to explain my integrated design practice.

Who is the intended audience?

The portfolio is aimed for potential employers as well as anyone who wants to know about my work.

What tone do you want for this site's written content?

The content is intended to be presentable and formal. In terms of tone, I would like it to be professional yet authentic to my own voice as a designer.

What will be the primary content of your site?

The primary content of my site will be my work. I will ideally have a home page with a menu that will separate different categories of my work. I would like to include three main sections: graphic design, product design, and fine arts.

What will the secondary content?

The secondary content of my site will invole an about me section, and a contact section.

How will users navigate through your site?

I want my site navigation to be quite simple. I want the user to always be able to the homepage from any page, and that the pages menu is clear. I want to create a drop down menu or a bar menu that could allow the user to switch between pages and design sections.
